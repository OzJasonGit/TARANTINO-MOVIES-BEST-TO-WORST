Tarantino Movies Ranked Best to Worst

The Definitive List By Oz Nwachukwu

Quentin Tarantino might be best known for his visceral, often gratuitously violent directing style. However, I prefer to pay homage to his inventive dialogue. His well crafted prose often find a sense of poetry in otherwise seemingly ordinary conversations.

Really, this is what this top 10 is about besides great story, directing and acting. Afterall, without the precocious dialogue the violence in Tarantino movies wouldn't be half as fun.

Not everyone's going to agree with this list... I don't care.
